Abstract

Water shortage in Egypt has appeared frequently and noticeably, especially in therecent period. Therefore, Egypt suffers from many grand challenges, includingoverpopulation, pollution, and health problems. The solution will solve this problemby providing another source of clean water by purifying the seawater byelectrodialysis. The design requirements that were chosen for the solution issustainability and effectiveness. Graphite was put in a container and connecteditsrods with trans, which give it uninterrupted electric current, the graphite rods turningto cathode and anode, the attraction of Cl2-, BR2- to the anode, and some elements found in seawater such as H+ and Na2+ attraction to the cathode. Then, using thesyringe, the water is drawn from the middle to be purer.The test was done more thanonce for precise results and using a TDS device to measure dissolved salts in water.The TDS of the water before the process is 35300ppm. The first test's result is34500pmm, results of the second test are 27500ppm. The TDS was decreased by ten.The primary conclusion from the solution and prior solutions is treatment andpurifying the seawater by modern, inexpensive techniques to increase clean water
